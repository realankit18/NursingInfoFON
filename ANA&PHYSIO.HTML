Anatomy & Physiology

Anatomy & Physiology is the branch of biology that studies the structure and function of the human body. Anatomy focuses on the physical structure, while physiology explains how these structures work together to support life.

Andreas Vesalius, known as the father of modern anatomy, revolutionized the study of human body structures in the 16th century.
